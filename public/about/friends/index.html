Communities

MtF.wiki

MtF.wiki is a website made by Project Trans (project-trans.org) for transgender indivuals seeking guidance.

Queering the map

Queering the map (queeringthemap.com) is a website where you can drop down pins with your own text on a Google-Map like interface to share your queer experiences. This can be anything from simple dates to just coming out. This map is important to remember that everywhere in this world, queer people exist. And that it’s not just a select few parts on the planet.

Anti-Dysphoria
